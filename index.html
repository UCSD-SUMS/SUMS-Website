Society of Undergraduate Mathematics Students (SUMS)

Purpose

SUMS, a UC San Diego student organization, strives to serve UCSD's mathematics community and department by promoting mathematics and related fields and industries. SUMS hosts talks, panels, technical workshops, professional events, advising, and social events in a collaboration among like-minded students, faculty, and staff.

Info

  • Our events and activities are publicized on our newsletter, on our Facebook group, and on flyers across campus.
  • Links to our newsletter, Facebook group, and Slack team are located at the top-right.
  • Meeting agendas and minutes are posted on Slack, our internal communication channel.
  • Our email is sums@ucsd.edu.
